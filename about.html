Vivekanandha Nursery & Primary School Founded on 4th June 2009, our school has been a pioneer in introducing structured daily routines in the district, ensuring that children start their day with discipline, focus, and joy. Beyond academics, we offer a wide range of co-curricular activities including Silambam, dance, yoga, and much more, allowing students to explore their talents and grow holistically.

We take pride in fostering a nurturing and inspiring environment where every child feels valued, confident, and encouraged to express themselves. Our dedicated teachers guide students not just in learning, but in building discipline, creativity, teamwork, and respect.
