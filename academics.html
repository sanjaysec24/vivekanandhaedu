Pre-KG

🧸 Learning through play: Children explore and learn in a joyful environment where play is the main focus. Activities are designed to make learning fun and natural.

📖 Storytelling sessions: Daily storytelling builds listening skills, imagination, and language development. It also introduces them to values, emotions, and simple life lessons.

LKG & UKG

🚀 Preparation for primary school: LKG & UKG build a strong foundation in language, math, and life skills — ensuring a smooth transition to Grade 1 and beyond.

🔤 Phonics and language development: Daily phonics sessions help children understand the sounds of letters, improve pronunciation, and build a strong foundation for reading and speaking.

Grade 1

✏️ Introduction to reading, writing, and basic arithmetic through fun and interactive methods.

🌱 Focus on building confidence, curiosity, and strong learning habits.

Grade 2

📖 Strengthening language skills, simple grammar, and number concepts.

🎨 Creative activities, storytelling, and projects to enhance imagination.

Grade 3

🧮 Introduction to multiplication, division, and structured sentence formation.

🌍 Learning about the environment, community, and basic science concepts.

Grade 4

📚 Advanced reading comprehension, grammar, and problem-solving skills.

🧠 Hands-on learning through experiments, models, and real-life applications.

Grade 5

🧾 Foundation for middle school with structured academics and critical thinking.

🌟 Leadership, teamwork, and communication skills through group activities and projects.
